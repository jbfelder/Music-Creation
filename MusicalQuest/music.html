Welcome to the Musical Creation!

For this game, you will need to use the full power of your imagination in order to create unique sounds and songs. If you want more of a challenge, there are also two types of 'Simon Says'. All games and sounds come from two genres: Electro and HipHop. Whenever you are ready, choose one of the following options:

ELECTRO - LB or HIP-HOP - RB or TUTORIAL - XBOX BUTTON
